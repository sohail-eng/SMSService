SMS Service

Bridge your messages through your Android device seamlessly.

How to Connect

1
Download APK

Get our latest Android application to enable bridging.

Download v1.0.0
2
Install & Open

Install the APK on your Android device and launch it.

3
Get Unique Key

The app will display a unique key. Note this down for sending messages.

4
Keep App Running

Ensure the app stays active in the background to process incoming SMS requests.

Send a Message

Integrate with REST API

Integrate this service directly into your own applications using our simple endpoint.

Endpoint: POST /sms/api/send

curl -X POST http://localhost:5000/sms/api/send \
-H "Content-Type: application/json" \
-d '{
  "unique_key": "YOUR_KEY",
  "phone_number": "03451234567",
  "message": "Hello from API!"
}'
